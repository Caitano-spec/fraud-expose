Fake News Lessons

In this section you will learn what's fake news, how to spot the difference between fake news and real news. By the end of this lesson you will become a pro in spotting fake news There is also a fun quiz towards the end which will help you learn and test your knowledge about Fake News.

What is Fake News ?

Fake news is false or misleading information that is presented as news. There are many reasons people post fake news either to damage someone's reputation, make money or just as a joke. Watch the video down below to know more about fake news

Different Types of Fake News

Below are few examples fake news and how they are viewed.

  1. Clickbait

  2. Propoganda

  3. Satire/parody

  4. Sloppy journalism

  5. Misleading headings

  6. Manipulation

  7. Rumor Mill

  8. Misinformation

  9. Media Bias

  10. Audience Bias

  11. Content farm

How to Identify Fake News ?

There are also websites like Snopes which help in detecting fake news online. Universities and Big Companies are creating their own software to do so aswell.
